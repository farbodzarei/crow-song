# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

People with **bodies in transition** seeking one-on-one therapeutic Ashtanga, not a studio class:
recovering from injury, surgery, or a long pause; carrying stress the body won't put down;
rebuilding strength gently; moving through pregnancy, postpartum, or age; experienced
practitioners wanting attention a led room can't give; and people curious about Ashtanga but
wary of its punishing reputation. They arrive cautious, a little vulnerable, looking for a
practitioner they can trust — calm and competence, not hype.

## Product Purpose

A single-page site for **Crow Song Yoga Therapy** (christine white). It exists to reframe
Ashtanga as what its Primary Series was always named — *Yoga Chikitsa*, yoga therapy — and to
move the right visitor toward booking a one-on-one session. Success = the visitor feels the
calm, trusts christine, and reaches the booking action without friction.

## Brand Personality

Calm, grounded, quietly mystical. Confident, approachable, professional — not shouting, not
whispering. The crow moves between worlds (transformation, intuition, sacred messenger); the
song is breath (ujjayi). Three words: **calm · grounded · mystical**. The whole experience
should feel like an exhale.

## Anti-references

- Spa / wellness clichés: sage green, blush pink, script fonts, lotus/mandala icons, "namaste" voice.
- Generic yoga-studio sites: class schedules, timetables, drop-in pricing grids, bootcamp energy.
- Fitness / transformation-before-after framing.
- Editorial-magazine affectation: display-serif + italic + drop caps (wrong register here).
- SaaS templates: hero-metric blocks, identical icon-card grids, eyebrow + 01/02/03 on every section.

## Design Principles

1. **Restraint is the brand.** Lavender appears only in small intentional moments. Large surfaces are dark or light. The discipline is the premium.
2. **Motion like breath.** Slow, unhurried, breath-paced (0.9–1.4s, slow ease-out). Nothing fast, bouncy, or attention-grabbing.
3. **Tradition, reframed.** Lead with the insight — Ashtanga was healing first. Authority through the real lineage (Mysore method, Yoga Chikitsa), not wellness marketing.
4. **One-on-one intimacy.** "christine" stays lowercase. The work is personal, adapted, built around one body.
5. **Negative space is a feature.** Premium comes from room to breathe, not decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Body text ≥4.5:1, large text ≥3:1. Every animation has a `prefers-reduced-motion`
fallback. The custom cursor is fine-pointer only and never hides keyboard focus or the touch
cursor. Semantic landmarks, correct heading order, visible focus states, alt text on imagery.
